The next morning, Vayne rose, dressed, and hurried out get her day started. She felt the urgency of getting this mission started. All her training made her ready, but very anxious to be underway. Working her way through the morning crowds, she finally reached the stall she was looking for. Petra was right; it was very large. “Business must be much better since I was last here,” Vayne thought as she entered.

“Be right with you,” came from the back of the stall. The rich, rumbling charr voice was familiar, and Vayne smiled.

“I’ll be right here waiting,” Vayne replied.
