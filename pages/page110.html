“In two days. I am waiting for Trauma to get back from Lion’s Arch. Then we will take the next pact airship back to Fort Vandal, and get out into the field as soon as we can,” Vayne explained.

“Lion’s Arch? What is Trauma doing there?” Aura asked.

With a knowing smirk, Vayne only said, “He’s going to get guildies. Ones that have skills almost as good as yours.”

Nodding, Aura snapped his fingers, and his glamour reappeared. Winking at Vayne, Aura tossed her hair over her shoulders. “Two days. I have plenty of time to enjoy myself. Loosen up, Vayne. Let’s have some fun.”

“Let’s have fun, he says,” Vayne muttered as she followed the mesmer through the crowd. The fight growing outside would have to wait for the time being.
