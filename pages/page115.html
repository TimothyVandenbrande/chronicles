“Can’t say that I have, but then again, I rarely leave this office. Try near the center of town. I heard there were some folks hanging out there. Maybe it’s your friends.”

“Thanks, Grimur. I’ll check it out. Coffee was great as always,” Trauma said as he turned and left the building. Trauma crossed the bridge back towards the Eastern Ward. Passing the gateways to the other Tyrian cities, Trauma walked on towards the bank. The bank was shaped like an octopus, and it always made Trauma smile to know that Lion’s Arch had a built in Cthulhu. Looking around, he saw so many people crossing the square. After a while, Trauma grew tired of searching through all of the strangers. Walking across the plaza, he entered the crafting area. Tradesmen were hard at work making weapons and armor. Food prepared by apprentice chefs was being offered for sale. Trauma purchased a plate of truffle steak, and wandered over to a grassy spot near the mystic forge.
