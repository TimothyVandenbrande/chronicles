Glaring at Rock, he answered, “Nope. So where are you headed now?”

“Well, I’m going to head over to the Trading Post and buy some more things to put in the forge over there,” Rock said with a sheepish grin. “I’m feeling rather lucky at the moment.”

“Mind if I walk with you?”

“Of course not.”

The two men walked in silence towards the Trading Post. Trauma paced in slow circles near the entrance as Rock picked up his purchases. Scanning the ebbing crowds, he still saw no sign of the guildies that Vayne promised were here. When Rock was ready, they began the walk back towards the mystic forge. Quaggans splashed in a fountain near the crafting stations.
