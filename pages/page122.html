“Yo, Trauma. Hey, Trauma. Over here, Trauma,” came the voice.

Trauma turned towards the voice and saw before him a purple plant, well, sylvari. “Do I know you?” he asked.

“Really, Trauma? You’ve forgotten me already? I’m Ian, Ian Blinktread. Raven’s ward,” he answered. Grinning, he threw his hand up to give Trauma a high five.

Ignoring the hand, Trauma walked around Ian, and answered, “Owe, I do remember you. You are the little fucker who wouldn’t stop drawing a target on me the last time we were with Raven. How could I forget,” he finished with a grimace.
