Reaching out to her pets, she praised them for once again keeping her safe. “Good boys. Let’s keeping moving,” she said as she checked the sky. The afternoon was passing quickly.

Raven crossed into Brisban Wildlands as the sun was getting low in the sky. Up ahead, she spotted the campfire of the Seraphs assigned to patrol these woods. She knew the man in charge of this camp, and she was glad that she had made it before dark. Approaching the camp, she was stopped by a young guard.

“Stop right there, miss,” the guard called out.

“Can I help you, recruit?” Raven replied.
