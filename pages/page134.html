Ian fell asleep on Isomeik’s shoulder just as Aura snuggled up against Vayne’s shoulder. Trauma, Vayne, and Isomeik exchanged knowing glances as they stared in silence. It was going to be a long night.

 

The next morning, the group was awoken by the sounds of shouting crewman. Apparently, they were in Fort Vandal, and had slept through the ship’s landing. Gathering their things, they struggled off the ship. Vayne, always ready for anything, was alert and giving orders to those around her. As she exited the ship, she was met by her lieutenant.

“Good morning, ma’am. I trust your journey was pleasant, or at least productive,” the junior officer greeted Vayne, while eyeing the group behind her.

“Yes, Lieutenant, it was rather productive, and more pleasant that I had hoped for,” Vayne replied.
