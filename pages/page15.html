“Raven? Well, I’ll be damned. It is you. Come on, girl. Let’s get you settled. Recruit, remember this face. She is one of us. Make sure you treat her accordingly next time.”

“Yessir.”

Raven called to her pets and followed Glovill up the hill into camp. She stopped near the fire, and suddenly realized just how tired she was. Sitting on a stump near the fire, she patted beside her, waiting for her pets to settle before turning to Glovill.

“Can’t believe you are still out in these woods,” she said as he sat next to her.

“No one else will stay hard on these bandits like I will, and you know it,” he said as he winked at her.
