Chapter 2

Raven rose at first light, ran her fingers through her hair, and threw on her boots. Standing to grab her gear, she nudged her pets to get moving. Grabbing breakfast from the rucksack, she chewed as she fed them, and got them water from a nearby bucket. Satisfied that they were ready, she thanked the closest guard, and asked him to once again tell the Watchman how grateful she was. “Let’s go boys. The sooner we get moving, the sooner we find everyone.”

The path towards Hidden Lake was easy to follow but known to be full of bandits and raptors. Raven hurried as fast as she dared without alerting animal or human alike to her presence. The tactic was working fairly well until Sparklefly got the idea to chase a rabbit into the bushes. “Sparklefly, get your ass back here. You crazy wyvern. Where are you going now?” Raven hissed after her pet.
