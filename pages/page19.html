“Come on, ya big baby. We’re almost there. I’ll let you chase Dam’s golem. How about that?” Raven chuckled as he seemed to like this idea. “Come boys.”

The rest of the morning passed quickly, and Raven soon saw the ridge that marked the entrance to Hidden Lake. Working her way around the breeze riders, careful not to disturb them, she soon saw Seraph Finlay at his post.

“Aidem, how are ya?” she called to him.

“Raven? Is that you?” he replied.

Laughing as she reached him, she answered, “Do you know any other ranger bouncing around with these kinda rascals?” as she motioned to her pets.

“No, can’t say that I do. I’m good. How are you? Come looking for Dam, have you?” he queried.
