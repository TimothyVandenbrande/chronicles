“Aidem, how are ya?” she called to him.

“Raven? Is that you?” he replied.

Laughing as she reached him, she answered, “Do you know any other ranger bouncing around with these kinda rascals?” motioning to her pets.

“No, can’t say that I do. I’m good. How are you? Come looking for Dam, haven’t you?”

“I’m good, and yes, I miss him and the others. Have you seen either of the other two?”

“Well, I heard tell that Vayne got stationed at Vandal’s Claim, on the edge of the Wastes, but I haven’t heard about Trauma lately. Sorry.”

“Thanks, Aidem. I’m glad you know that much. Say hi to the wife and kids. I need to find me a couple wayward necros,” she smiled as she walked further into the camp.
