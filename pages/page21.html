Raven watched the hustle and bustle of the Seraph along the lake. Guards were patrolling everywhere, skirmishing with bandits who tried to sneak into camp. Sparklefly was chasing blue moas as Nevermore dipped and dived in the sunlight. Reaching the edge of the main camp, Raven heard a laugh that she had sorely missed.

“Ha ha ha. Looking to spend some coin?” she heard the familiar voice say.

“You break it, I break you,” she tossed in reply, watching as his head turned to her.

“Howdy, toots. Bout time you got here,” he said.

“Hiya, dear. Missed you too,” she smiled. She sat down on the log beside him as Sparklefly charged him, knocking him off the log, licking his face like an excited puppy. “Think someone else missed ya too,” she grinned.
