Raven chuckled as she set out beside him, Sparklefly nipping at the heels of the golem, in search of Trauma. They knew they needed their leader back if they were going to deal with whatever threat was looming.

 

They made it to The Gallowfields in the middle of the afternoon. There was no immediate sign of Trauma, but they were hopeful that he was still here. “Nevermore, find Trauma,” Raven whispered to the bird as they took a break for water.

“Dam, you think he is really still here?”

“Dunno, but I do know that wherever he is, he’s fine. Stop worrying,” he said as he searched her face. “He’s Trauma.”

“You’re right. Let’s keep looking.”
