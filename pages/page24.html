Another hour of walking passed before they heard a noise in the distance. Holding up her hand, they stopped to listen. The area was known for bandits, and they didn’t want to walk into a trap. An ambush could prove fatal this far out. Easing closer, they both soon began to chuckle as they recognized the sound.

“Goddam rats! Fuckers! That’s not ‘shinies.’ That is junk. The fuckers!”

Grinning from ear to ear, Raven turned to Dam, “Think we found our missing Trauma.”

“Sounds like it.”

Hurrying forward, they found Trauma knee deep in junk and skritt corpses.

“TRAUMA!!” Raven scolded as she saw the heap at Trauma’s feet.
