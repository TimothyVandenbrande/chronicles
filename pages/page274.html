Wrapping an arm around her, Trauma pulled her against him and whispered in her ear, "When you are ready, find me." Letting her go, he stood and turned to go.

"Don't stay here long, Raven. It's not safe, and you are needed in camp," he said.

"Yessir," Raven stated to his retreating figure. Rocking from side to side, Raven gave one more look below before standing silently and heading towards the center of the town. Barely noticing the bonfires of corpses, she shuffled back to join the others, mechanically eating her dinner, but not noticing anyone or anything around her.
