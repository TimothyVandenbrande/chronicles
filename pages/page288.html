Offering a small smile, Raven nodded. "Thanks for understanding, Trauma," she said hoarsely. "Go check on them. I'll be fine." Nodding, Trauma left the tavern. Raven placed her chin on her hands and stared into space as she tried to gather herself. It was going to be a long road ahead.

 

Tarr found Raven sitting outside of the tavern, dangling her feet over the edge of the platform. "Don't jump, Raven," he teased her as he came closer.

"Holy shit! You almost made me jump! Geez, you scared me!" she exclaimed.

"Sorry, Raven. I wasn't trying to scare ya. I was wondering if we could send Nevermore with the messages now. I know it will take some time, and I don't want to waste any," Tarr finished.
