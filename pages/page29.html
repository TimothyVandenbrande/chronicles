Walking into Vandal’s Claim, they must have been a sight. Two men covered in blood from head to toe--their golems marching alongside--followed by a woman and her animals. The Seraph on tower duty called down to them.

“State your business,” he commanded.

“We’re here to see Vayne. She knows us. Tell her Raven is here,” came the reply.

“Wait right there,” the guard said as he disappeared to relay the message.
