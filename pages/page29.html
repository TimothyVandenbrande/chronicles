Walking into Vandal’s Claim, they must have been a sight. Two men covered in blood from head to toe, their golems marching alongside, with a woman and her animals. The Seraph on tower duty called down to them.

“State your business.”

“We’re here to see Vayne. She knows us. Tell her Raven is here,” she tossed in reply.

“Wait right there.”

“Raven? You’re here?” Vayne called from inside the fort.

“Yeah, it’s me. I brought some presents for ya,” Raven chuckled as she eyed the bloody men.

“Really? What did you bring me…” she stopped as she spotted the group. “Ah, I see what you brought me. Trouble.”
