Trixie showed up the next day. After saying hello to everyone, she followed Raven to a spot along the edge of the platform. The two women sat for quite some time together as Raven told her what happened to Mondo. Trixie hugged Raven as they both cried for their fallen friend. While they were away, Trauma took the opportunity to tell the others about Mondo as well. The guildies glanced towards the women, and then dropped their heads as they thought about what Trauma had said. Murmuring among themselves, they soon straightened as Trixie and Raven joined them. Tarr reached down and softly patted Trixie on the head as she stood before him. Grabbing his paw, she returned his affection, looking up at him and nodding. She turned back to look at Trauma as he spoke.

"Now that we're all here..." Trauma began.

"Excuse me. Excuse me. I hope I'm not late," came a voice outside the tavern.
