“Nice to see you too, Vayne,” Trauma said with a chuckle. “I see you still haven’t learned a real profession.”

“Well, some things never change, do they, Trauma?” Vayne tossed, but a smile began to tug at the edge of her lips. “Come in. Let’s get you cleaned up, and you can tell me why you’re here.”

Later that evening, the foursome sat around a table in Vayne’s office. She was assigned to command Fort Vandal until reinforcements made it. Her exploits in the Wastes were well known, and she was well respected among the Orders. She looked around the table and realized just how much she has missed her friends while they had been apart. They were like family, and family should stick together.

“So what is it? Something brought you guys to my door,” Vayne finally asked.
