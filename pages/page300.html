Chapter 11

The next morning, they were up before the first pale streaks of light crossed the mountains. Excitement and nervousness about what lie ahead had them ready to leave. Those going quickly grabbed their things, and said goodbye to their friends. This might be the last time they were all together, but no one pointed that out. Exchanging final hugs, Trixie and Raven nodded to one another as the group began to walk away. Following the others up the stone stairs to the magical portal, Raven straightened her shoulders, and prepared herself for the journey ahead. At the portal, they stepped through one at a time, and waited for the rest on the other side. Once they were all through, Trauma looked to Raven.

"Do you know the best way to proceed?" he asked.
