Before he could ask, Raven said, "I have never been this far before. We are headed south to find Mordemoth. This path leads south and it seems harmless. Let's just continue as a group until something tells me otherwise, ok?"

Nodding, Trauma motioned for her to lead the way. The rest of the guildies fell in behind the two, and were silent as they continued up the path. At the top, they found a large clearing. Aside from plants, a few wild boars and moas, there was nothing impressive about the area. Coming to the edge of the clearing, they stopped. They were at the edge of yet another sheer rock face. There seemed to be more land below them, but it was hard to make out in the mist.

"Don't worry, I'll go. I'll send Nevermore back up if it's safe," Raven said.

"Take one of your scouting party," Trauma said.
