Trailing behind Raven, they entered a small passageway. Rocks and plants were all that could be seen on either side. They walked single file because there was no room to walk any other way. They were not in there long before they spotted light from the other side. Soon they came out into an open area. A wooden pole stood before them with a lit torch atop it. Proceeding slowly, they came to another land bridge, a torch on either side of the opening. Crossing the bridge, they spotted a clearing with a couple burning fires and crates scattered everywhere. They spotted a sleeping sylvari on a mat. Beside her was an asura washing her forehead. Trauma moved forward to address the asura.

"Excuse me, but are you Pact?" he asked cautiously.
