She motioned for them to join her at the campfire. Moving around to face her, they sat across the fire from her. Able to see her clearly now, Raven was taken aback by her appearance. Her skin was pale, so pale that it glowed in the light of the fire. Her hair was pulled on top of her head. It was black, but not. There was something strange about it. A jeweled hair piece caressed her forehead just above pale blue eyes. They were as cold and dead as ice, but enchanting all the same. Her traveling dress was black with a white waistcoat. An axe made of ice and shaped like a dragon along with a leather whip lay at her feet. Raven suddenly realized what the mist surrounding the stranger was.
