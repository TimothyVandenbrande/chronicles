"The Luminate has agreed to see you. In fact, she seemed rather excited to learn that you were here. She has asked that I escort you to the city as quickly as possible. Are you and your guild ready to leave?" Kengo asked.

"Yes, we are. Just lead the way, and we will follow you," Trauma said.

"Excellent. Let us depart then. It is not a long walk and you are most anxiously awaited."

Nodding, Trauma motioned the others to fall in behind. They followed the exalted down the path away from the pylon and into the jungle. They did not know what was in store for them in the city, but they were ready to face it. After a short walk, the exalted pointed to an archway. The group saw light through the archway, but could not make out anything else. "Welcome to Tarir, Cthulhu Dawn. You are our honored guests. I hope that you find everything that you are looking for."
