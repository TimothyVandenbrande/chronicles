Chapter 12

The group followed Kengo down the stairs into Tarir. They opened out onto a golden terrace. Trees were visible in front of them, and they walked to the edge to take in the view. They gasped at what they saw. A panoramic view of a golden city lay before them. Waterfalls splashed down from the rock walls that hid the city from the rest of the jungle. Trees, vines, and plants grew all around making it a lush oasis even with all the golden architecture.

"How do we get down there?" Aura asked breathlessly.

"There are many ways to get down to the center of the city, but we are not going there first," Burnisher Kengo said. "I am instructed to bring you immediately to the Luminate, and I will do so. Come, we must hurry."
