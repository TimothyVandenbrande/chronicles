Up two small sets of stairs to the right, the exalted stopped at a mushroom.

"Have you ever used one of these?" she asked.

"I can't say that we have," Trauma replied. "What is it?"

"It's a means of getting around. Well, for creatures that still have legs," the exalted corrected. "It is simple really. You merely climb on it, and bounce gently. The air inside of the mushroom will do the rest. Careful with your landing," she said as she disappeared again.
