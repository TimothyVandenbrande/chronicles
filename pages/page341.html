"Okay, go ahead and test it. We'll watch to make sure you are safe," he agreed.

Nodding, Raven stripped off her boots and bags of gear. Leaving her bow behind, she took only her sword and warhorn for protection. Swimming across the small pool, she climbed the bank and approached the marking. Nodding to the guild, she whispered, "Here goes nothing," and touched the mark. Instantly she was teleported to the other side of the magical wall. She waved at her friends through the wall to let them know she was safe. Then padding down the path in her bare feet, she began to look around. At the end of the path, she found a golden archway with a magical portal in the center. It shimmered exactly like the one to their guild hall. Curious about where it led, she knew better than to go alone. Turning around, she went back to the wall and touched the marking on this side. It took her back to her friends. She swam across to them and relayed what she saw.
