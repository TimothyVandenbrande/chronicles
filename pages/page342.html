"I know we would have more sleeping space in the golden hall over there, but I think it is best that we swim over tonight so we have time to dry out," Raven said as she stood dripping before them. "I don't think we want to explore soaking wet. The leather in my armor is already starting to chaff," she continued with a grimace. I would like to be dry if I need to fight stuff." The others agreed and began stripping off their boots and other things that would weigh them down. "I have an idea. Can I borrow some of your swords? I want to create a raft to float across our bags. Don't want the food and things wet that shouldn't be."
