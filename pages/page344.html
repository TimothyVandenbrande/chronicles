Passing looks among themselves, they realized she was right and quickly stripped down--except for Iso. Just as she had the day they went swimming, she stayed fully clothed to dry.

"I hope Ian is ok tonight. It is worrisome that he hears the voice here. Hopefully, we can find the cause of these strange occurrences and be on our way soon," Trauma said. The others nodded. They were all very silent tonight. Talking didn't seem appropriate, and they were all wondering what they would find beyond the magical portal tomorrow. Once everyone was dry and back in their gear, they settled in for the night. Sparklefly snored gently beside Raven who tossed and whimpered in her sleep.
