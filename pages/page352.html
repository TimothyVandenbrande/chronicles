Chapter 13

Once they were all through the portal, they took a moment to look around. Aura was right--most of the entrance was water and bridges, but they could barely make out some sort of buildings in the distance. The stone beneath their feet was the same golden material that Tarir was made from.

"Did we just find the exalted's basement?" Daeron joked.

"I'm not sure, but we are wasting time," Trauma said. "Let's move on. Aura, which way did you go before?"

"Across the bridges in front of us. It's kinda the only way I could find short of swimming," she finished with a giggle.
