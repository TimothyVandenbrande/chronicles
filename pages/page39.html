Turning to Trauma, he gave his friend a nod. Trauma returned the nod with a certainty of the understanding between them.

“See ya later,” Dam said.

“Yup,” Trauma replied.

With that, Dam left the fort, and headed across Brisban. It was going to take him several days to make it to Snowden Drifts, and he wanted to get there as soon as he could. After a few hours, he spotted a group of breeze riders floating along the edge of some ledges. He tossed rocks at them, and laughed as they chased him. He quickly lost them, but was still amused at his own antics. Reaching the Seraph camp just before dark, Glovill welcomed him with a smile.

“Hey, what brings you this way? Raven with you?” he asked Dam as they sat around the fire.
