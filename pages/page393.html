“Then lead the way. We’ll trust your judgement. Just don’t walk us off a cliff,” Trauma finished with a grin.

“Haven’t found one yet, so I think you’re safe for a bit,” Raven replied, winking at him. “Let’s keep moving. The sun is starting to get a little low, and I don’t see a nice place to sleep yet.” The group fell in line behind Raven, and they started down the new path. They hadn’t gone far when the path forked again. Raven didn’t even hesitate this time, and immediately took the left fork. Suddenly something launched itself at the group. Mushrooms like they had found before the guild hall were jumping all around them. They quickly killed them and stared at the pile of corpses on the ground.

“Maybe we can have roasted mushrooms for dinner,” Dam suggested.
