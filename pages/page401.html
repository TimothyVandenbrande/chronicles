Rolling onto her side, Raven watched the others slowly trickle to bed as she grew sleepy. Sparklefly curled against her back, and it was a familiar, comforting feeling. Listening to Nevermore preen his feathers as he settled in for the night also brought Raven comfort. It was the little things that brought her the most happiness. Maybe that is why she found so much joy in Cthulhu Dawn—because it was the little things about the people around her that she held so dear. Smiling with this thought, she finally drifted off to sleep.
