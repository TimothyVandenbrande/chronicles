Iso and Likoi were sparring with their daggers in the flattened area beyond the fire. Blood sat, as Raven did, feeding her pets. It all seemed so quiet and peaceful. Too quiet for Raven’s tastes. She didn’t want more fighting, but after what they had been through, she knew it was inevitable. What they had fought before was nothing compared to what they faced, and that worried Raven. She’d already lost one friend, and she was afraid that there would be more gone before it was all over.

Looking around her, she stopped and studied each of her friends, and thought of all the reasons that person was special to her. Vayne always gave her the scoop on anything and everything, and she considered her a good friend. Aura often made her laugh with some party trick.
