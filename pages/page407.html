“You know, this place is beautiful, even if everything in here wants to kill us,” Raven said.

“Indeed,” Trauma said. “The view is stunning, but I would rather not waste time staring at our surroundings. I don’t know about you guys, but I am anxious to kill a dragon.”

Murmured agreement could be heard all around, and so they continued on their way. Following the only available path, they went left and made their way along an exposed tree root. The root was large enough for several of them to walk side by side without falling off. As they reached the end of the root, Raven spotted another marker. One solitary rock stood on the root right before it went back into the ground. Pointing to the rock, Raven continued past it and hopped down onto the ground. Ten meters later, she found an arrow, made from river rocks, pointing towards a cave.

“Seems we are going the right way,” Vayne said.
