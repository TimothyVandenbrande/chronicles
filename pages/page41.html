Dam crossed the border into Kessex Hills, and made his way passed Fort Salma. Soon he spotted clouds of green drifting in the air. Sniffing, he realized that it was poison, and he made his way carefully around the area. By noon, he was glad to see that he was making good progress, and his journey was going smoothly. Once he reached Earthlord Gap, Dam decided it was best he stopped for a quick bite to eat. Just as he was munching on a piece of dried meat, he felt hot breath on his neck, and turned to see a centaur staring at him. Slowly chewing as he stared at the centaur, he swallowed and asked, “You need something?”

“You, puny human, are trespassing on Tamini land. I should kill you where you stand,” came the reply.
