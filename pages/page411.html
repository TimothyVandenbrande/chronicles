“I will not be eating those either. In fact, after this trip, I may become a vegetarian,” Raven said.

“Eh, not taking this for food. They smell too bad,” Dam said with a grin.

Continuing through the cave, they had to slosh through a shallow part of the stream to get back to dry land. A tree root ran through much of the section, making it easier not to get soaked. Climbing over another tree root, they made it back to land, and immediately found another marker. This arrow pointed farther into the cave. There was a well-worn path leading into the cave.

“I wonder who or what traveled or travels through here enough to make such a clear path,” Trauma said.
