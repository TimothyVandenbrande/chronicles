Up ahead, the path forked, and each way looked well used. The right fork continued ahead on level ground, disappearing around a tree root. The left fork, quickly came to a dead end at a stream. More bugs loitered around the water. Chopping them to bits, the group turned to stare at the running water.

“I guess we are going right, then,” Alex spoke up softly.

“Not so fast,” Vayne replied. “The stream has to flow somewhere. Maybe it holds a better way.”

“Hmmm, maybe. Let me check over here. I want to see if I can tell where the stream is headed,” Raven said as she followed the bank along the creek. Hopping onto a rock even with the water, Raven jumped from rock to rock until she reached the bank further downstream.
