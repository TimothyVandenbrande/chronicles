“Look at these things,” he said excitedly. “What do you think they are?” he asked as he pointed to a circular pit filled with a shiny blue goo that seemed to pulse.

“No idea. Let’s find out,” Trauma said as he sliced it open with his greatsword. Inside was a very small version of the bugs they kept encountering.

“I think it is an egg,” Raven spoke up. “Looks like you just killed the baby buggy.”

“In that case, I want to smash every one of these that we find along the way. Much as I love killing shit, I think the less of these things, the better,” Trauma ordered. “Something tells me we should go right,” he said looking at Raven.

“Then that something agrees with my something,” she said. “I don’t know why I think we should go right, but my gut is pointing in that direction.”
