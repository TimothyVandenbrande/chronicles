“I wonder if these structures are natural or some other force made them?” Vayne said. “It is hard to tell because they seem so well laid out. Strange.”

Continuing along the rock bridges, they soon found a ledge that looked like a broken bridge. Across the gap was a ledge extending towards them, but the whole center of what would be the bridge was missing. Looking around they noticed that they could glide to a dry section of the path below them. They had made it past the water and bugs, and could continue that way.
