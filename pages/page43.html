There was still so much to be done, and it felt like not enough time for it all. The rest of the afternoon passed quickly and quietly. Dam reached Black Haven just as twilight fell. Lionguard Micah showed Dam a place he could sleep for the night, and bid him safe travels for the coming days. As he drifted off to sleep, he wondered just what he would find tomorrow.

 

Leaving Black Haven, Dam crossed the border. Skirting the Lawen Ponds, a swamp known to be full of undead, he passed First Haven, and continued on. There was so much open land here, and not a soul around for miles. His morning passed uneventfully, and he soon found himself in Cornucopian Fields, a small farming community just outside of Lion’s Arch. Strolling through the town, he looked for a small inn.
