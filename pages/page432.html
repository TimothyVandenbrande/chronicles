Chapter 16

In the morning, Trauma awoke to a figure standing over him. Rubbing his eyes, he looked again.

“About time you woke up,” Petter said cheerfully. “I’ve been waiting for hours.”

“Hours? Really?” Trauma asked in disbelief.

“Nah, not really hours, but glad you are up. We’ve got a dragon to kill,” Petter said as he waited for Trauma to get up.

“I’m coming. I’m coming,” Trauma said. “Go wake the others while I get reacquainted with the sunshine,” Trauma grumbled.

“Will do,” Petter said with a salute as he moved off
