Hammers, maces, shields and spears lined the rack in neat rows. “Must be the handy work of the blacksmiths,” Raven thought aloud. “No one around here know how to make a bow?” she chuckled as she continued exploring. On her left was the tent housing the blacksmiths and their supplies. An engine or generator from a crashed Pact ship was chugging along. Raven noticed lines running from the machine to the lights they had seen the night before. “Clever,” she mumbled. Deciding that she had explored enough without the others, Raven walked back to their camp. Trauma was just gathering the others.

“Ah, Raven, there you are,” Trauma said as he caught sight of her.

“Yeah, sorry. Just roamed through the camp a bit to stretch my legs. What’s up?” she asked.
