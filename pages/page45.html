Dam crossed the border into Kessex Hills, and made his way passed Fort Salma. He skirted Greyhoof Meadows, careful to avoid the poison clouds that still sprang up in the area. By noon, he was glad to see that he was halfway through Kessex Hills, and his journey was going smoothly. Once he reached Earthlord Gap, Dam decided it was best he stopped for a quick bite to eat. Just as he was munching on a piece of dried meat, he felt hot breath on his neck, and turned to see a centaur staring at him. Slowly chewing as he stared at the centaur, he swallowed and asked, “You need something?”

“You, puny human, are trespassing on Tamini land. I should kill you where you stand,” came the reply.
