“Indeed,” Trauma said as he pushed himself away from the rock wall. “Let’s go, people!” he called to the others. Alex helped Tarr to his feet, and the group stood waiting to leave. Not far past the camp, the trail sloped upward and disappeared around to the left. Coming around the corner, they found another mordrem patrol. Petter quickly dispatched two of them, and Iso stabbed the last one. Alex burned the bodies, and the group moved on down the path. Killing a second set of modrem, the guild followed the terrain, sloping down into a small tunnel created by large twisted vines. Reaching an archway, Trauma motioned for them to stop. Looking ahead, they saw a small ledge running a circle around a large pit. In the middle of the pit was an island. On the island stood a huge mordrem unlike any other they had seen.

“I think we found the pit,” Petter said studying his surroundings.
