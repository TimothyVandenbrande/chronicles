Chapter 17

Trauma told the others how Aura had managed to disable the mordrem production, and that they only had a small window before their large friend in the middle noticed. “We’re just going to have to move quickly and take that thing down,” Trauma said. “Any questions or suggestions before we go?” No one said a word, so Trauma turned to Raven. “You take point on this one. You have the most range, and can create a diversion if we need one.” Nodding she turned towards the entrance and waited for the others to join her. Dam fell in beside her like always, and she drew her bow. This was it—no going back after making this creature mad. Mordemoth would surely know they were here, and Raven was nervous. Taking a deep breath, she turned to the group and asked, “Ready?”

“Ready!” came the reply as the others drew their weapons and created a tight formation behind her.
