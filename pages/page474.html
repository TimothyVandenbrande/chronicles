“Nice work, Petter,” Trauma said. “Grab your things, people. We’ve got a dragon to slay. Petter, lead the way, please.”

Taking point, Petter waited for everyone to fall in behind him. Leading the way down the bridge, Petter took them across the ledge to the entrance he found earlier. “This way,” he said as he pointed to a narrow entrance. The path was small but not claustrophobic. Following the twists and turns of the trail, it soon opened up to the edge of a cliff. The group stood on the edge taking in the view before them. In front of them were islands made of rock. Suspended in mid-air, the islands were huge, and unmoving. There was no way of knowing how many islands hung in the wide expanse before them.

“How the hell are we supposed to get over there? Are we even supposed to get over there?” Iso asked.

“You see those ‘clouds’ over there? The tall ones?” Raven asked.
