Mistress. He worked his way through the jungle beyond the Silverwastes. Several days passed before he made it to the cliff before the guild hall. Jumping, he quickly began gliding and drifted his way to the opening. He wondered if anyone had arrived before him. He hadn’t stepped foot in the guild hall since they left the others in charge. He had sent word back that Mordremoth was dead, and the others could depart if they wished. Soon, he landed, made his way to the portal, and slipped through. Once through the other side, he almost walked head first into someone. Looking down, he smiled.

“Hiya, Trauma,” Raven grinned as she hugged her friend. “How’s you?”

“Good,” he replied. “How are you?”

“Better now,” she smiled. “Ian is running around the guild hall leaving clones, and chasing Alex. Daeron is roaming around checking things out. Not sure there are many others here yet.”
