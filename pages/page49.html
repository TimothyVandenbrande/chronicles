“Well, keep up the good work,” Dam said with a grin. “Think I’ll catch a little sleep before I have to leave in the morning. Give Almorra my best. Thanks for the place to stay.”

The next morning, Dam awoke to a chill in the air. Searching his pack for an extra layer, he threw on the gear and headed out. Crossing the rugged terrain, he finally made it over the border into Snowden Drifts. The snow covering the ground was slowing him down. He stopped at the first lodge he saw, and double checked directions up to Scholar’s Cleft. He didn’t want lost in the snow at dark.
