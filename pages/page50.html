Stepping close to her, he whispered, “Vayne sent me. About Petter.”

Nodding her understanding, she led him to a small table tucked into the corner. It was hidden from view, and away from listening ears.

“So Vayne sent you, huh?” she asked as they sat down. “How do I know she really sent you? You could be trying to trick me.

“I could be, but I’m not. Look, time is wasting. Vayne told me you would ask me a question that only she could give the answer to. Ask your question, and see if I am telling the truth.”

“Ok, where was I on the eighth day of last month?” Chaylse said with a grin.

“Easy. Beggar’s Burrow,” he answered quickly.
