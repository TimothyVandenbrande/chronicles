He admired the pinkish color of their leaves against the blinding white snow. Even as the sun was setting, they were still a sight to behold. Continuing up the path, he entered the small courtyard. There were even more of the cherry trees here, making the courtyard feel like a safe haven.

Just passed the courtyard was a small room with a fire going. A table and benches ringed the room, with bookcases against the wall. Standing in front of the fire, Dam stretched out his hands to thaw from the cold. Scholar Elyse noticed the stranger, and went to introduce herself.

“Hello, I’m Scholar Elyse. I work here at Scholar’s Cleft. Can I assist you in anyway?”

“Hello, I’m Dam Almavet. I’m here looking for shelter for the night, and also for a friend of mine—Tibbia. She came here for research, I am told,” he answered.
