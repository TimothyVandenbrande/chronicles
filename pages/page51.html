Surprised by his answer, she whispered, “Ok, you are the real deal. What do you want to know?”

“Where’s Petter? How can I get to him? I need to see him, now.”

“Well, I can’t tell you where Petter is. I’m sorry, but it’s not allowed. I can’t even tell Vayne where Petter is. Petter is in service to Queen Jennah, and that is all you need to know about it. He gave me a message for you. He said to tell you that he is always watching. He knows about the situation, and he can’t come right now. He did say that someone named Trauma would know when to call, and that when called, he would come,” she finished in a hurried whisper.

Finishing the last sip of his beer, he nodded. “Thanks for the information. Tell Petter I said be safe out there, and be waiting for our call.” He rose from the table, leaving a few coins next to his empty mug. Grabbing his bag, he walked out of the inn without looking back.
