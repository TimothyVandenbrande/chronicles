The afternoon sun was dipping low, and he still had a walk ahead of him. Turning right, he followed the road until it forked near Newbeach Bluffs. Going left at the fork, he made his way passed the lurking minotaurs and reached Vigil Keep as the stars began to glow. Warmaster Yosif stopped him at the door of the headquarters.

State your business, stranger,” he commanded.

“No stranger here, Yosif. Just Dam. I wanted to speak to Almorra tonight before I turned in,” Dam answered.

Dam! How are ya? Good to see ya. Sorry, but Almorra is already in bed for the night. Is it important? I can wake her.”

Nah, let the old woman rest her bones. I just wondered if any of my guildies have been through here lately. Seems I need to find them.”
