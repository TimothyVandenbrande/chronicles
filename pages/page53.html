“You are welcome. Stay as long as you like. If you need anything, just ask. Good night,” she smiled as she turned to go.

“Goodnight,” Dam said to her retreating figure. He noticed a small fire had been made in the room, and the bed was ready for him. “These Priory folk think of everything, I guess,” he thought as he set his bags in the corner. Sitting on the edge of the bed, he removed his boots and extra layers. Setting them aside, he laid down on the bed, and quickly fell into a dreamless sleep.
