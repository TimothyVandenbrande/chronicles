Running his fingers through his mohawk, he answered, “Of course, I am. Now let’s get some sleep. Morning comes early, and you’re right, we need to move out as soon as we can. Good night.”

“Night,” she said as she lay back against the pillow. Staring at the ceiling, she wondered if it would all be enough. Crossing her fingers at her side, she thought about her friends and hoped they were making progress as well. “The guild needs to be whole again,” she thought as she drifted off to sleep.

 

Raven awoke to the wonderful smell of food. Rubbing her eyes, she sat up in bed and saw Mondo seated quietly munching on food, feeding her pets in between bites. Grinning, she teased her friend, “I go to sleep and this is what happens, huh? You win the hearts of my pets with food?”
