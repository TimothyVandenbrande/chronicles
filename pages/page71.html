They stopped briefly at the Altar Brook Trading Post and grabbed some supplies. They were careful to stick to the road and avoid Scaver Plateau. Soon the sun was passed its zenith, and they realized their stomachs were growling…or at least Sparklefly’s stomach was growling very loudly. They came to a fork in the road, and stopped to read the signpost. “I know where we are,” Raven said aloud. That’s Eldvin Monastery. They make their own wine here. It’s a very lovely place.”

“Wine you say?” Mondo asked. The leaves of his mohawk seemed to perk at the mere mention. “Think they would mind if we popped in and had lunch there?”
