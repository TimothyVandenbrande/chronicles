“Yes, ma’am. Fights are held in the Pit. In fact, if you and your friend hang around long enough tomorrow, you can see one. The weekly match is tomorrow.”

“Thanks for the information, soldier. We’ll check into that.” Raven nodded her head in thanks to the guard and turned to Mondo. “So let’s grab a spot and get some sleep. We can sort it all out in the morning. Whadda say?”

“Sounds like a plan,” Mondo replied as they left the post in search of the Pit. They quickly found a tent near the campfire that seemed unoccupied. Using their sacks for pillows, they propped up facing the tent opening, the pets at their feet. Too exhausted to chat, they quickly fell into a deep sleep.

 

Morning came early as the camp was abuzz with activity even before dawn.
