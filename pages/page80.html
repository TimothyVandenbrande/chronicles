Looking around, they quickly found a place to cross. Careful not to slip on the wet rocks, they were soon over the stream. A few hundred yards in front of them was the entrance to a cave.

“Is this our cave? We didn’t have to go very far, if so,” Raven said.

“Appears to be it, but one soldier said it is a very large cave, so who knows how far we’ll have to go,” Mondo replied. “Let’s get going. The sooner we find them, the better.”

“Agreed.”

The pair crossed the remaining distance to the cave entrance, and slowly entered. It was fairly well lit for a cave, and Raven became more cautious. They quickly found the origin of the stream they had crossed. It was fed by a small waterfall not far into the cave. They heard a low rumble and small rocks fell across their arms and shoulders.
