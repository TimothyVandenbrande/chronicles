It was very flexible, and Raven wasn’t sure if it was friend or foe.

“Mondo, honey, what is that thing?” she asked.

“That ‘thing’ is a translucent ooze,” came the reply, but it wasn’t Mondo’s voice. Turning they saw two people approaching, a charr and an asura. Raven thought she recognized the charr, but the asura was a stranger.

“Who are you?” she asked as they approached.

“I am Tarr the Unbent, and this is my companion, Likoi. I could ask the same of you. Who are you?”

“I’m Raven, and this is Mondo. We’re friends of Boar’s from Cthulhu Dawn, and we have been looking for you,” Raven answered as she reseated her arrow into her quiver.
