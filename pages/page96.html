Running his fingers through his hair, Trauma pauses. “I think I’ll find a place to stay for the night. I have a feeling I will need the rest before I find them,” he said as he arched an eyebrow at Vayne.

Stifling a giggle, she said, “That might be a good idea.”

They felt the airship begin to descend near the city. Standing patiently, they waited until the crewman gave the signal to depart. Vayne stopped to thank the captain once more, and made sure he would have room for her to return to Fort Vandal in a few days. The airship had to land outside the city in Queensdale, so Trauma and Vayne walked through the village of Shaemoor until they reached the gates of Divinity’s Reach.

Once inside, Trauma turned to Vayne. “I think I will go on to Lion’s Arch tonight. I can stay at the inn there. Then find those guys in the morning. Easier time through the gates tonight as well.”
